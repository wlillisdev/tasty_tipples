About Us

Firstly, Welcome to Tasty Tipples.
We are a group of friends who set up Tasty Tipples back in the great lock down of 2021. When the pubs closed and the off licences were jammed, we said Hey.... lets sell direct to the customer via an online drink shop, and so it all began.

We are passionate about everything artisanal, including beer, whiskey, tequila, wine, and gin. Do we really need to say more? Our aim is to bring unique, creative drinks right to your home. The drinks industry is always evolving, and our committed team is constantly looking for the newest trends, enticing beverages, and ingredients to create any cocktail imaginable.

Mission Statement
'To support all Microbreweries and distillers in Ireland and promote their products to both the local Irish Market and on the world stage via our website'
Tom Tipple...C.E.O Tasty Tipples

Our aim is to deliver an awsome customer experience and our expert, enthusiastic team members are delighted to share their knowledge and help customers to find their perfect tipple.
